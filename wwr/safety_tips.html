Saftey Tips:

  • First and probably the most important is listening to your guide!
  • Follow where your guide leads you, they know the way!
  • Always wear your life jacket and helmet they will protect you if you end up in the water.
  • Hold your paddle properly and with a firm grip.
  • Learn to swim should probably go without saying. If you don't already know how, take some lessons beforehand.
  • Never venture alone. Always have a buddy or group to aid you in any situation.
  • Research the area in case you get seperated from your buddy/group.
  • If you fall out of the boat don't try to stand up! Your foot could get stuck and that's a dangerous situation to be in.
  • If you're in water don't panic, and determine if the boat or the riverbank is closer.
